Privacy Policy

At Data Fusion, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

  1. Information Collection:
           We collect personal information when you use our platform, including your name, email address, and any other data you provide through forms, surveys, or sign-ins. We also collect non-personal information such as usage data and browser details.
  2. Use of Information:
           We use the collected information to improve our services, respond to inquiries, send updates, and personalize your experience. We may also use your data for analytics and to monitor the platform's performance.
  3. Data Sharing:
           We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your personal data may be shared with trusted partners or service providers who assist us in delivering our services, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  4. Data Security:
           We take the security of your personal data seriously and employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  5. Cookies:
           Our platform uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide a personalized service. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  6. Data Retention:
           We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
  7. Your Rights:
           You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You may also opt out of certain data collection practices, such as marketing communications.
  8. Children's Privacy:
           Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.
  9. Policy Changes:
           We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the most recent update will be indicated at the top.
  10. Contact Us:
           If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@fuse_data.com.
